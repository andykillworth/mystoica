Symbolic Notes
A — The Court of Towne Square
A stolen thing arrives at an address named for a square it will never reach — receiving is the crime of standing still while the world's motion catches up to you.
B — The Welfare Check
A welfare check is a question asked of a body that cannot yet answer; the intersection is not incidental, it is the exact point where two lines of travel cross and one of them goes quiet.
C — The Salmon Upstream
Salmon are built to travel against the current toward a fixed point; a contaminated batch is a migration interrupted mid-river, memory curdling before it reaches its spawning ground.

Each exhibit is a different species of arrested motion — a stolen object at rest, a body checked for signs of continuing, a fish recalled before it can complete its run — and together they describe the same single verb: to stop before arrival.

Working Theory — The Adjacency Clause

Under the standing Adjacency Clause, any three public records filed within the same seventy-two-hour window, regardless of jurisdiction, are treated as co-authored by a single unnamed custodial hand moving between desks it is not permitted to name. The Clause does not require geographic adjacency — it requires only that each record involve an interruption of transit: a person, a package, or a product caught mid-route and forced to answer for itself. Towne Square, Bleecker and Third, and a smoked-salmon tub in an Irish cold-store are, under this reading, three windows onto the same corridor, lit at different hours.

The Cascade

Traced forward, the custody chain moves from property to person to product, tightening with each transfer.

1

Owensboro, Kentucky — A stolen object crosses into the wrong hands at Towne Square Court and is logged, formally, as received rather than taken — the first admission that motion has stopped.

2

The file is dated for a Monday, the day of the week custody chains traditionally reset, when weekend transit becomes weekday record.

3

Utica, New York — Two hundred miles northeast, at the same class of hour the previous morning, a body at a named intersection is checked for the same thing the Kentucky record checks for property: whether it is still in motion.

4

The welfare check clears as 'in progress' — an unresolved status that mirrors the undeclared allergen and unconfirmed contamination language of a food recall filed the same week.

5

Ireland — Across the ocean, a batch of smoked salmon en route to dinner tables is recalled mid-transit, its final leg of travel — like the stolen property, like the welfare check — arrested before completion.

Nothing here traveled its full distance; the custody chain simply records where each thing was standing when someone finally looked.
