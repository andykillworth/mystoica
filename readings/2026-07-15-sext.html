Symbolic Notes
A — The Overflow
A counting system that runs out of room does not stop; it begins again under a new face. The 50 that replaces the 99 is not a new number so much as a forced confession that the old one was always going to end.
B — The Unwitnessed Meadow
A field named for abundance holds a call logged for abuse — the place that promises pasture and quiet instead files a wound. The name and the record disagree, and the record wins.
C — The Sweetness With a Foreign Body
The raisin is dressed in chocolate specifically so that whatever else is inside it can pass unnoticed. The recall exists because the label lied by omission, not by falsehood.

Each exhibit is a container that failed to disclose its true contents — a serial number, a meadow, a candy shell — and each failure was only caught because someone downstream kept a record precise enough to notice the gap.

Working Theory — The Adjacency Clause

Under the Adjacency Clause, no record filed within the same 72-hour custody window as another is truly unrelated — proximity in the filing stream is itself a form of authorship. The Clause holds that a bureaucratic reset, a hidden wound in a meadow, and a hidden nut in a raisin are not three stories but one story wearing three jurisdictions, staggered across clocks that have not yet noticed they are keeping the same time.
