Working Theory — The Adjacency Clause

We continue to work under the Adjacency Clause: any two records logged within the same shared radius of time and theme are bound by custodial threads whether or not their authors ever meet. This week the radius was breath itself — legal breath (a child's residency), mechanical breath (a diver's air supply), and atmospheric breath (a house's interior air) — three guardianships of the same substance, filed by three unrelated hands within a five-day window, none aware they were completing the same figure.

The Cascade

Trace the custody chain forward and it does not stay in one jurisdiction for long.
